## Important structural note: shared negative lists

Some services we've been blocking as negatives (teeth whitening, composite bonding, root canal, crowns, bridges, bone graft) are actually DBD services. The shared negative lists were built for the IMPLANT campaigns where these terms are waste. 

**Solution:** When building the Cosmetic campaign, DON'T attach all 11 shared lists. Only attach:
- Location 1 WORD "phrase" (always)
- Competitors & Brands "phrase" + [exact] (always)
- 1 WORD "phrase" — BUT review for conflicts first (e.g. "whitening" may be in there)

Each campaign gets a tailored set of shared lists, not a blanket attach-all approach.

## Match type strategy

- **Phrase match:** Primary match type for all keywords. Catches long-tail variations.
- **Exact match:** Add on top 3-5 highest volume terms per ad group for tighter control.
- **Broad match:** Not used. Too risky.

---

## Ad strategy per ad group

- **3 RSAs per ad group** (Google recommends 3 minimum for testing)
- **15 headlines + 4 descriptions per RSA**
- **Headline 1 pinned** = ad group theme keyword (e.g. "All On 4 Dental Implants")
- **Ad group keywords MUST appear in headlines** for Quality Score + Ad Strength
- **Descriptions max 86 characters** (safety margin under 90 limit)
- **Consistent USPs across all ads:** Hammersmith, Lifetime Warranty, Vivo Bridge®, Free Consultation (pending Giulio's answer), 0% Finance
- **No unverified claims** until Giulio confirms (99.12%, Best Implant Clinic 2024, etc.)
