Every weekly report follows this exact slide order:

| 8 | **8-Week Context** | Trend view | 2 line charts side-by-side (Weekly Spend + Weekly Conversions) |
| 9 | **Client Clarifications & Actions** *(or Decisions Needed)* | Responses to client or questions for client | Two-column table — their input / your action |
| 10 | **Week N+1 Plan** | What comes next | Mon-Fri table: Day / Focus / Expected Output |

**Slide 9 variation:**
- If client has replied to questions during the week → title is "Client Clarifications & Actions"
- If there are pending decisions awaiting client → title is "Decisions Needed"
- If nothing pending → skip this slide and renumber
