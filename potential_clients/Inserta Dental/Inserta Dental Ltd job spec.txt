About the job
The Company

Prodent Group is a growing dental group combining:

A premium London clinic (Dental by Design)
Advanced laboratory capabilities (Vivo Dental Lab)
A network of partner clinics across the UK
The group works closely with its partner clinics, not only supplying implants and restorative solutions, but also supporting them in growing their practices and generating patients. Alongside this, Dental by Design operates in one of the most competitive areas of the UK market.

The Role

We are looking for a hands-on Performance Marketing Specialist to take ownership of paid acquisition across both the flagship clinic and partner network.

The focus of the role is to:

Generate high-quality patient enquiries
Continuously improve performance
You will manage campaigns across:

Google Ads (Search-led)
Meta
Operating in a highly competitive B2C environment where:

Search volumes are high
Competition is intense
Cost per click is significant
This is fundamentally different from lower-competition B2B campaigns. Success in this role requires the ability to:

Manage complex accounts
Interpret data accurately
Optimise performance on an ongoing basis
You will be responsible for:

Running campaigns directly within the platforms
Refining targeting, messaging and landing pages
Ensuring that marketing spend translates into real patient bookings rather than low-quality enquiries
This is not a strategy-only or agency management role.

You will be hands-on and accountable for performance.

What You Will Be Doing

Managing campaigns directly within Google Ads (with a strong emphasis on Search) and Meta, taking full ownership of performance and ongoing optimisation
Building and refining campaigns targeting high-intent patient searches, structuring accounts effectively and improving performance through continuous testing and optimisation
Working closely with landing pages and user journeys, identifying drop-off points and making adjustments to improve enquiry rates and overall conversion
Analysing performance data in detail, using platform insights and conversion data to make informed decisions
Improving cost per lead, conversion rates and lead quality over time
Managing campaigns across multiple clinics, adapting messaging, targeting and positioning depending on the services offered and the profile of each practice
The Candidate

We are looking for a true paid media specialist with a strong, in-depth understanding of paid advertising.

Hands-on experience managing Google Ads (particularly Search-led campaigns) and Meta within lead generation environments
Experience operating in highly competitive markets, not lower-competition B2B environments
Strong ability to analyse performance data, interpret campaign behaviour and identify improvements
Confidence working across targeting, bidding strategy, ad copy and landing page performance
Proven experience personally managing and optimising campaigns with measurable results
A key indicator for this role is the scale and complexity of campaigns you have worked on.

Candidates who have managed larger budgets will typically have:

Greater exposure to testing
Stronger optimisation experience
More familiarity with complex data
This role is not suited to:

Generalist marketers
Brand or content-focused profiles
How to Apply

Please submit your CV along with a brief summary of:

Campaigns you have managed
Platforms used
Budget levels
Results achieved (e.g. improvements in CPL, conversion rates or scaling performance)
