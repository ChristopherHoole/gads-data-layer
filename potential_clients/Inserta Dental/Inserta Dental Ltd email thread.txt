The Role
We are looking for a hands-on Performance Marketing Specialist to take ownership of paid acquisition across both the flagship clinic and partner network.
The focus of the role is to:
Generate high-quality patient enquiries
Continuously improve performance

Google Ads (Search-led)
Meta
Operating in a highly competitive B2C environment where:
Search volumes are high
Competition is intense
Cost per click is significant

Manage complex accounts
Interpret data accurately
Optimise performance on an ongoing basis

Running campaigns directly within the platforms
Refining targeting, messaging and landing pages
Ensuring that marketing spend translates into real patient bookings rather than low-quality enquiries
This is not a strategy-only or agency management role.
You will be hands-on and accountable for performance.
What You Will Be Doing
Managing campaigns directly within Google Ads (with a strong emphasis on Search) and Meta, taking full ownership of performance and ongoing optimisation
Building and refining campaigns targeting high-intent patient searches, structuring accounts effectively and improving performance through continuous testing and optimisation
Working closely with landing pages and user journeys, identifying drop-off points and making adjustments to improve enquiry rates and overall conversion
Analysing performance data in detail, using platform insights and conversion data to make informed decisions
Improving cost per lead, conversion rates and lead quality over time
Managing campaigns across multiple clinics, adapting messaging, targeting and positioning depending on the services offered and the profile of each practice
