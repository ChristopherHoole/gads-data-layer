### 6. Ad Strength Optimisation Learnings
Initial ads scored Average/Good. Through iterative testing discovered:
- **Headlines 1-10 must be literal keyword phrases** from the ad group for Quality Score
- **Max 4-5 headlines starting with same words** — vary opening words for uniqueness
- **Include 2-3 broader popular keywords** alongside specific ad group terms (e.g. "dental implants", "teeth implants", "dental implants london")
- **Each description must start with a different keyword phrase**
- **Descriptions: min 75 chars, max 86 chars** (Google's 90 limit minus safety margin)
- **Headlines: strict max 30 chars** (several caught at 31-32 during build)

### 7. Low Search Volume Keyword Cleanup
Paused 60 keywords flagged as "Not eligible — rarely served" across 12 ad groups. Worst hit: Dental Implants Hammersmith (11 keywords — hyper-local postcode terms too niche) and Pay Monthly (9 keywords — too many long-tail payment rephrasing).

All 4 legacy ad groups paused. All 27 legacy keywords confirmed covered by new ad group structure.

## Key learnings (for ACT build)

1. **Ad strength requires literal keyword inclusion** — headlines must BE the keywords, not just reference them. Rule: 10 keyword headlines + 5 USP headlines per RSA.
2. **Headline uniqueness check** — max 4-5 headlines starting with same words or Google flags "not unique enough".
3. **Description character sweet spot** — 75-86 characters (under 90 limit but long enough for Google to value).
4. **Low search volume keywords** should be paused immediately — they dilute ad group relevance and may drag ad strength scores down.
5. **Legacy ad group pausing** — don't delete, pause. Preserves QS history. Confirm all keywords are covered by new structure before pausing.
6. **Healthcare policy on dental keywords** — expect "Health in personalised advertising" policy flags on every new dental ad group. Request exceptions immediately, they get approved within 1-3 days for established accounts.
7. **Shared negative lists need campaign-specific attachment** — services blocked as negatives for implant campaigns (whitening, bonding, root canal) are actual services for cosmetic/general campaigns. Plan list management before launching new campaigns.
