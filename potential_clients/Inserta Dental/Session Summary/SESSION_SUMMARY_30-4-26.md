## 11. Process learnings banked

- **Auto-generated assets ARE a real issue on legacy campaigns** — DII had 264 of them, all Limited. New campaigns post-toggle don't have this problem. Worth a quarterly audit.
- **Phrase match negative mechanics are ambiguous** in Google's docs — words-between behaviour not explicitly tested in Google's own examples. Defaulted to exact-match for risky variants until verified live.
- **Teeth Fixing ad group concept failed** — Google's close-variant matching treats "fix teeth" as semantically equivalent to "dentist". Won't work for implant-intent campaigns. Banked for future campaign design: avoid generic "fix" verbs as keyword roots.
- **Health in personalised advertising is structural** for UK dental advertising — sitelinks/promotion/Price assets WILL flag Limited regardless of wording. Best path: accept the limit on sitelinks, build callouts using generic-Brand patterns to stay Eligible.
- **DBD has Custom Interest signal asset built and saved** — apply to other PMax/DSA campaigns if launched in future.
- **Don't make assumptions about policy violations or claim agreements without source** — Giulio email re-read confirmed he's NOT asking to remove banned claims (99.12%, save 60%, voted #1, top 10 Europe) — those are Chris's call vs reach-vs-claim trade-off.
