## Method notes (for ACT build)

- **"Disable auto-apply first"** — must be step 1 in any ACT onboarding. Google's auto-apply silently undoes strategic work.
- **Campaign-level negatives need match-type checks** — single-word negatives added as exact are safe; as phrase/broad they can kill a campaign. ACT needs to flag match types when reviewing campaign-level negatives.
- **Classifier logic for search term processing** — substring matching for DBD service exclusion, not exact. Found today after "all on 4 dental implants cost" got incorrectly blocked.
- **Bid strategy pattern:** Max Conv Value + tROAS → Target CPA (with data-driven target below current CPA) is the standard fix for accounts optimising on fake conversion values. This pattern likely applies to most new client onboardings.
- **Postcode-level geo structure** with borough fallbacks is the reusable London-local geo template for DBD and should be the ACT default for London clients.
