# Master Sub-Group Plan

**Headline: 74 total ad groups proposed across 26 parents.**
**Plus 842 TOURISM keywords go into defensive negs across all groups.**

Generated by `plan_subgroups.py` against the 16-month audit DB.

Each parent ad group is broken into intent dimensions. Sub-groups marked **BUILD** become standalone ad groups; **MERGE_INTO_CORE** rolls back into the parent's Core sub-group; **NEG** terms become defensive negatives.

Split rules:
- TOURISM = NEG always (defensive negs across all groups)
- < 3 keywords = MERGE_INTO_CORE (not enough volume to justify own ad group)
- bookings > 0 = BUILD (proven conversion, deserves own focused RSA + LP messaging)
- >= 10 keywords AND >= £50 historical spend = BUILD (volume justifies)
- else MERGE_INTO_CORE
