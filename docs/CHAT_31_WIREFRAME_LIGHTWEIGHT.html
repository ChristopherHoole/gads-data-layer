FAQ

Common questions.

What size accounts do you work with?
£5k–£100k/month ad spend. You need enough budget for the data to be meaningful and for A.C.T's insights to have impact.
Is A.C.T a SaaS product I can buy?
No. A.C.T is my internal tool. It's my competitive advantage, not a product for sale. You get access through working with me.
How long is onboarding?
2 weeks. Account audit, tracking review, structure fixes, strategy alignment, then we're live. We fix the foundation before pushing spend.
Can I see A.C.T in action?
Yes. I demo it on the fit call. You'll see the dashboard, recommendations, change log, and reporting live.
What do you NOT do?
Social ads, SEO, creative production, landing page design. Google Ads only. I'm a specialist, not a generalist.
How does pricing work?
10 hours/week block rate. We discuss on the fit call based on account complexity, number of campaigns, and current state.
What if A.C.T recommends something I disagree with?
You have final say. A.C.T recommends, you decide. If you reject a recommendation, I'll explain the reasoning and we'll discuss alternatives. No blind automation.
How quickly will I see results?
Account cleanup and structure fixes: 2-4 weeks. Performance improvements: 4-8 weeks minimum (we need statistical significance). This isn't magic — it's rigorous testing and optimization.
What happens if you reach 4 clients?
I maintain a short waitlist. Average client retention is 12+ months, so slots open periodically. If there's urgency, we can discuss interim audit/consulting work.
Can you work with my existing agency or team?
Yes. I work as freelancer (direct), partner (collaborative), or white-label (behind the scenes). Flexible models depending on your setup.
